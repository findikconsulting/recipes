Ingredients:
 - 3 eggs
 - 1 tomato
 - 1 onion
 - 1 green pepper

Directions:
  1. In a medium saucepan, melt butter.
  2. Cut the onion in small cubes and sautee on the pan.
  3. Cut the pepper also in cubes and mix and sautee on the pan with onions.
  3. Cut the tomato in cubes and mix with rest in pan and cook for couple minutes.
  4. Add the eggs in the pan and mix and cook for 1 minute.
